PLEASE DON'T PASTE IN TEXT FROM A WORD-PROCESSOR
------------------------------------------------
The recommended way is to make your file in Notepad
or some other plain-text editor, save it as a backup,
then copy from there and paste into the box below.

The rules are:

- An oucu on a line on its own is assumed to be a tutor.
- No commas or anything but the oucu allowed on a tutor line.
- The oucus which come after a tutor are assumed to be students.
- Students' oucus should be separated by commas

When you have finished, you can click on the 'preview' button to
check that the students will be assigned correctly to their tutors.

When happy with the result: click the 'submit' button.

Example, with two tutors hgd4 and ask3 and ten students:
--------------------------------------------------------

hgd4

asf3, lkj3, asd33, lkjs34

ask3

ascf3, lckj3, ascd33, lkcjs34, ascf3, lckj3
